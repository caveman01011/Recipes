Chocolate Latte

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 1 tablespoon heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • ⅛ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup brewed espresso
  • ¼ cup white chocolate chips, chopped

Steps

  1. Whisk milk and cream together in a saucepan over high heat until hot and frothy. Off heat, stir in sugar and vanilla extract.
  2. Whisk hot espresso and white chocolate chips together in a mug until smooth. (If you are making two lattes, pour half into another mug.) Top with hot frothy milk; stir to combine.
